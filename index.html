About Me

My ardent love for Computer Science pushed me to pursue it as a bachelor's degree. In due course of building projects, I found where my happiness was. I loved creating and bringing ideas to life. This is the rush that I seek to get as a developer. The numerous contests I've won and my streak on Hackerrank are a testimony to the excitement that coding brings to me. This is what got me into the tech giant, Amazon. I have been working for 8 months now as an SAE intern. Amazon has played a huge role in moulding me from a developer to a true professional.

Back in college, I've published a paper in an international journal and I've worked on three other projects. I've attended over 15+ workshops in a bid to explore the different domains in CS. Apart from learning extensively, I also love to help others code. That's how I became the CTO of iCode, an entrepreneurial venture in my campus, where apart from carrying out my duties as the CTO, I also taught Python to over 20 students.

I do believe that there are miles to go and this journey of reinvention and pursuing my passion is possible only at a place that has the same values as mine. I am looking for opportunities as a developer in such a company.
